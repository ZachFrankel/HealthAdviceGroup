Common Health Conditions & Prevention

Seasonal Allergies

Common Symptoms:

  • Sneezing and nasal congestion
  • Watery or itchy eyes
  • Runny nose
  • Itchy throat and ears
  • Fatigue

Prevention Tips:

Indoor Prevention

  • Use air purifiers with HEPA filters
  • Keep windows closed during high pollen times
  • Regular house cleaning and dusting
  • Wash bedding weekly in hot water

Outdoor Prevention

  • Check daily pollen forecasts
  • Wear sunglasses outdoors
  • Shower after outdoor activities
  • Avoid peak pollen times (early morning)

General Health Tips

Stay Hydrated

  • Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily
  • Monitor urine color
  • Increase intake during exercise
  • Carry a water bottle

Exercise Regularly

  • 30 minutes daily activity
  • Mix cardio and strength training
  • Take regular breaks from sitting
  • Stretch daily

Mental Wellness

  • Practice daily meditation
  • Maintain social connections
  • Get adequate sleep
  • Manage stress levels
